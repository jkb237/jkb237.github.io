About me


    I am a Mechanical Engineer with a drive to get things done - a spirit which I gained from years of working on both Cornell's Electric and Combustion powered Formula SAE cars. Thanks to my FSAE experience, I am completely at home when taking on new and novel technical challenges, and I have four strong years of practice integrating mechanical, fluid, and electrical systems. I am hoping to take that experience to a team of dedicated engineers working on meaningful projects.

    Technical interests aside, I like to spend time in nature - actively and relaxing. I enjoy climbing rocks, camping, kayaking, and just hearing the sounds of the outdoors. I'm always looking for new things to do outside, and I love picking up new skills.
